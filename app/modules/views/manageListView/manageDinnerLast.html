Scallion Pie

Description

A Chinese savory, unleavened flatbread folded with oil and minced scallions, it is made from dough instead of batter.

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour
1-1⁄4 chopped green onions - cleaned and patted dry
1 cup boiling water
3 tablespoons Chinese toasted sesame oil
1⁄4 - 1⁄2    salt
1⁄2-1    spoons vegetable oil

Hot Pot   

Description

Places meat and vegetables into a a simmering metal pot, the cooked food is usually eaten with a dipping sauce.

Ingredients

10 cups rich seafood or chicken stock
1 tablespoon ginger, minced
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
6 green onions, chopped finely
2 tablespoons sugar
3⁄4 cup dried mustard powder
2 tablespoons sesame oil

2⁄3 cup rice wine vinegar
12 shrimp, peeled, deveined and butterflied
